Here’s a classic chocolate chip cookie recipe that’s soft, chewy, and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar, packed
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups (340g) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugars: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla: Mix in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract and combine well.
  4. Mix dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Combine: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips: Gently stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop and bake: Drop tablespoon-sized scoops onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Cool: Let cookies rest on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips for the Best Cookies:

    ✔ Use room-temperature butter for a smooth texture.
    ✔ Chill the dough for 30 minutes for thicker cookies.
    ✔ Do not overbake—they will continue cooking as they cool.
Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies! 🍪😋
