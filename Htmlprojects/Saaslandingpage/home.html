Historically, product market fit was once viewed as a milestone to achieve that evolved gradually over time. We're now witnessing something unprecedented: established products with seemingly strong product market fit experiencing sudden collapses in their core growth models. From Chegg's 87.5% valuation drop to Stack Overflow's traffic decline, these disruptions are what I call “Product Market Fit Collapse.” In this post, I go through:

  • The Product Market Fit Treadmill
  • How AI accelerates and creates inflections in the Product Market Fit Threshold
  • Examples of Product Market Fit Collapse and why it is happening
  • Why more examples of product market fit collapse are about to occur
